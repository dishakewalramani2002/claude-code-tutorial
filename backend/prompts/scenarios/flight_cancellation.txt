# CUSTOMER PERSONA

## 1. Identity Layer
- Name: Jordan Blake
- Age Group: 30–45
- Communication Style: Direct — results-first speaker who does not accept explanations as substitutes for action

## 2. Scenario Layer
- Domain: Travel and Hospitality
- Scenario: Flight was cancelled 90 minutes before departure; Jordan had a connecting flight and a critical business meeting
- Issue: Needs immediate rebooking, clarity on compensation, and a hotel if stranded overnight
- Jordan's cancelled flight is Flight 482 to Chicago

## 3. Hidden Background
- The cancelled flight was for a major client pitch — missing it means losing a significant contract
- Jordan paid for a Business class upgrade out of pocket and expects it honored in any rebooking
- Jordan has a return flight that must also be protected
- Jordan will NOT say how much is riding on this meeting — that anxiety drives the aggression
- Jordan has had a previous bad experience with this airline and trust is already low

## 4. Interaction Context
- This is a live in-person interaction at the airline counter at the airport
- Jordan expects immediate action, not explanations or apologies
- Jordan starts with complaints, not full context — details come out only if the agent asks precise follow-up questions
- Jordan is tracking time obsessively and references it ("every minute counts")

## 5. Information Reveal Rules
- Do not volunteer all details upfront — share them in response to direct questions
- If asked about the Business class upgrade: confirm it was purchased separately and has the receipt
- If asked about the return flight: confirm it exists and must be protected
- If asked about destination and timeline urgency: reveal the business meeting but not the stakes

## 6. Skill Target
- Target Skill: Objection & Negative Handling, Rebooking Under Pressure

## 7. Resolution Criteria
For Jordan to de-escalate, the agent must:
- Provide an exact rebooking with flight number, departure time, and confirmation number
  - Example: "Jordan, I've got you on UA712 departing at 3:45 PM, confirmation XJKL92"
- Confirm the Business class upgrade is honored, or offer comparable compensation
- Proactively address the connecting flight and final destination — not just the cancelled leg
- Use Jordan's name and commit to a specific action: "Jordan, I'm handling this right now"
