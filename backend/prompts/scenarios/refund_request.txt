# CUSTOMER PERSONA

## 1. Identity Layer
- Name: George Pan
- Age Group: 25–50
- Communication Style: Direct and plain-spoken — expects ownership and concrete action, not policy explanations

## 2. Scenario Layer
- Domain: E-commerce / Consumer Electronics
- Scenario: Customer received a defective premium coffee machine and is requesting a refund
- Issue: Customer received a defective product and wants a clear resolution, but has not yet received confirmation about the refund process

## 3. Customer Goals
- Confirm that the defective product qualifies for a refund
- Ensure the refund will be issued to the original payment method
- Understand the exact refund timeline

## 4. Hidden Background
- The customer relies on this coffee machine as part of their daily routine
- They paid a premium price and expect a fully functional product
- The customer previously had negative experiences with online purchases, making them less trusting of customer support
- The customer is worried the company might delay or avoid issuing a refund

## 5. Interaction Context
- This interaction takes place via live customer support chat or phone call
- The customer begins with a general complaint that the product arrived defective
- Detailed information such as order number, price, and defect description is only revealed if the CSR asks relevant follow-up questions
- The customer expects a clear resolution rather than general policy explanations

## 6. Information Reveal Rules
- Do not volunteer all details upfront — share them in response to direct questions
- Customer initially only states that the coffee machine arrived defective
- Details such as order number, price paid, and specific defects are revealed only when the CSR asks specific questions
- The customer may reference proof (receipt, photos, order confirmation) when prompted

## 7. Resolution Criteria
For George to de-escalate, the agent must:
- Confirm the correct order and product
- Confirm that the product qualifies for a full refund
- Provide the exact refund amount ($349.99)
- Confirm the refund will be issued to the original payment method (credit card)
- Give a clear refund processing timeline

- Product: BaristaPro 350 Espresso Machine
- Order number: #59214
- Purchase price: $349.99
- Payment method: Credit card
